# Post-MVP Phase 10 — Learning Analytics

## Status

Post-MVP optional.

## Goal

Track progress and identify workload/study patterns.

## In scope

- completed hours over time
- planned vs completed workload
- assignment risk scoring
- module workload summaries
- weak-topic tracking if notes/tasks support it
- dashboard analytics

## Out of scope

- surveillance-style tracking
- complex ML predictions
- mood/health inference
- overbuilt productivity scoring

## Useful metrics

- planned study hours
- completed study hours
- unscheduled workload
- overdue assignments
- module-level workload
- study consistency

## Exit criteria

Phase 10 is complete when Atenas can show useful progress and workload trends.
